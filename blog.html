What is Iraqi Women Rights Organization Missions ?

Our mission is to help women to get know, get their rights and build a new generation with strong knowledge about their role in Iraqi/middle eastern society.

Empathies women role in building new society and highlight these issues to males to correct/bring attention to.

Our mission is to:

1.Establish laws against underage marriages, unlawful marriages (Imam marriage) and to support civil marriage.
2.Establish laws against women and child abuse/ violence.
3.Stand against the “covering system” of Iraqi women exclusively.
4.Develop a law to give mothers the right to issue passports for their children.
5.Provide daycare centers and strengthen the child protection law.

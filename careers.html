Project Assistant

Core Functions / Responsibilities:

1-Undertake cultural orientation activities in an assigned area or areas, such as such as training, scheduling or childcare and, as required, guide and assist in monitoring teams of Project.
2-Undertake cultural orientation scheduling activities for RSC MENA/ CO Unit, including, if assigned, providing all individuals requiring cultural orientation classes the opportunity.
4-Assist in overseeing cultural orientation childcare activities for RSC MENA/CO Unit, including, if assigned, monitoring and guiding staff members providing childcare services.

Context:

IOM Cultural Orientation unit in MENA also offers Cultural Orientation training to individuals resettling to Australia, the United Kingdom, Switzerland, Italy, France, Spain, Germany and few other European countries throughout the region.

#OURWHY

Girls Education

According to UNESCO, 26.4% of Iraqi Women are illiterate, with the percentage believed to be much higher in rural areas, reaching up to 50%.

#OURWHY

Child Marriages

24% of girls in Iraq are married before the age of 18 and 5% are married before the age of 15. Child marriage is most prevalent in Al-Najaf (where 30% of women aged 20-49 were married before the age of 18)

#OURWHY

Domestic Violence

At least 36% of married women reported experiencing some form of psychological abuse from their husbands. 23% verbal abuse, 6% physical violence, and 9% violence.
